About Us

At our company, Poshak Group, we have over 25 years of experience and knowledge in the poultry industry. We are dedicated to providing high-quality products and services to our customers. Our company was established in 2001 and has since been involved in the poultry business.

At Poshak Group, we are proud to be involved in various aspects of the poultry industry. We specialize in poultry feed manufacturing, layer farms, broiler integration, breeder, and hatcheries. In 2018, we expanded our business by entering into the poultry medicines trading industry with the name of Poshak Medicines Pvt Ltd. We now deal in poultry health products, medicines, vaccines, and feed supplements to better serve our customers.

Our company has a wide reach, and we supply our products in Rajasthan, Haryana, Jammu, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and Maharashtra. We take pride in being a well-known name in the Gujarat poultry industry. Our reputation speaks for itself, and we are dedicated to providing the best products and services to our customers.

At Poshak Group, we are committed to providing a seamless experience for our customers. Our website is designed to make it easy for you to access our products and services. We believe in providing quality products at competitive prices, and we are confident that you will find what you are looking for on our website. Thank you for considering Poshak Group for your poultry needs.
